Speed Baccarat Rules

Goal: Bet on which hand (Player or Banker) will have a total closest to 9.

Card Values: A=1, 2-9=face value, 10/J/Q/K=0. Hand total = sum mod 10.

Naturals: 8 or 9 on first 2 cards = natural win/stand.

Third Card: Player stands on 6-7, draws on 0-5. Banker rules depend on Player's third card.
