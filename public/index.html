How to Play

1. Select one or more houses (A, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6) and place bets between ₹2,000 and ₹60,000 on each.

2. Priya (the dealer) shuffles the deck and draws 3 cards randomly.

3. If a card matches your house number, you win!

1 Match1:1 payout
2 Matches1:2 payout
3 Matches1:4 payout

Example: Bet ₹5,000 on house "A". If 2 of the 3 cards are Aces, you win ₹10,000.
